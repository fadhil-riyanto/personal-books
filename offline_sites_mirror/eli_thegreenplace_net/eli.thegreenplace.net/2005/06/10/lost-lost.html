lost "lost"

June 10, 2005 at 10:09 Tags Miscellanea

How big was my dissapointment after finishing watching the last episode of "Lost" upon finding out that this dumb series hasn't ended.

Where do I even get started ? "Lost" is terrible - it's unrealistic, inconsistent, fakely-dramatic, politically correct and what not. A crash on a mysterious island is just an excuse to show how f*cked up the lives of some people are. Every one has a life of a tele-novel, no less. No one normal... The dramas it tries to create are so obviously fake, they're funny, and the shallow & inconsistent interaction between the people on the island only make everything worse.

A reasonable acting is the only shining light in this terrible creation. And to name the last 3 chapters "Exodus" (a obviously "endish" name) and still not end it - argh.
